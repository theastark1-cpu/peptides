Injection Technique

  1. Wash hands thoroughly with soap and warm water.
  2. Wipe vial stopper with alcohol swab. Let dry completely (10–15 seconds).
  3. Draw air into syringe equal to dose volume. Inject air into vial to equalize pressure.
  4. Draw dose slowly. Flick to dislodge bubbles, expel air. Confirm exact dose at eye level.
  5. Wipe injection site with fresh alcohol swab. Let dry (wet alcohol stings).
  6. Pinch skin fold (1–2 inches). Insert at 45° (lean areas) or 90° (abdomen, love handles).
  7. Inject slowly: Small volumes (~5–15u): 5 seconds. Medium (~30u): 10 seconds. Large (100u+): 15–20 seconds.
  8. Withdraw needle, apply gentle pressure with swab. Do not massage (can accelerate absorption and cause irritation).
  9. Dispose immediately in sharps container. Never recap a used needle.
  10. Rotate sites: Abdomen, thighs, love handles, upper glutes. Avoid injecting same spot within 3 days.

Large-Volume Injections (≥100 units):

MOTS-c (100u), Pinealon (100u), and Glutathione (200u) are full-syringe injections. Use fattier sites (love handles, upper outer glute). Inject very slowly (20–30 seconds). For Glutathione at 200u: either use a 3 mL syringe with 25G needle (IM glute), or split across 2 insulin syringes at different sites. Warm the syringe in your hand for 30 seconds before injecting — reduces tissue resistance and discomfort.

Common Questions

Q: Why is Tesa/Ipa #1 when it doesn't score highest in any single category?

Because it's good at everything. Tesa/Ipa scores 50+ in 4 of 6 categories — no other peptide does that. It's the foundation the rest of the stack is built on. The specialty peptides (Semax for cognition, DSIP for sleep, MOTS-c for energy) dominate their individual lanes but contribute nothing elsewhere. The overall ranking reflects multi-goal utility, not single-goal potency.

Q: Can I combine injections to reduce the number of pokes?

Yes, with rules. Compatible in same syringe: Wolverine Stack (BPC+TB, already blended), Tesa/Ipa (already blended). Can combine: Semax + Selank (both nootropic class, similar pH). Never combine: Tesa/Ipa with anything else (requires fasted state, different pH sensitivity). MOTS-c (full syringe already). IGF-1 LR3 (requires precise small dosing, don't risk dilution). Glutathione (acidic — keep separate). Pinealon (gene expression peptide — keep separate). DSIP (already only 5 units — not worth combining).

Q: What happens if I miss a dose?

MOTS-c: Skip, resume next scheduled day. Don't double dose or shift the schedule. Semax: Skip, resume next day. IGF-1 LR3: Skip, resume next day. Never double dose. Tesa/Ipa: Skip, resume next scheduled day. Wolverine: Skip, resume next day. DSIP: Skip, resume next night. Selank: Take if within 2 hours of normal time; otherwise skip. Pinealon (during cycle): Resume next day; extend cycle by 1 day to maintain total dose count. Glutathione: Take next day — flexible scheduling.

Q: How long until I notice results?

Depends on the peptide and goal. Sleep (DSIP): 1–3 nights. Energy (MOTS-c): 1–2 weeks. Cognitive (Semax): Same day (acute). Body composition (Tesa/Ipa): 8–12 weeks for noticeable changes; 26 weeks for measurable VAT reduction. Cell improvement (Glutathione, Pinealon): Cumulative — track biomarkers, not subjective feel. Hair/skin: 30–90 days (hair cycles are slow). Tissue repair (Wolverine): Days for acute pain reduction; weeks-to-months for full structural repair.

Q: Is 9 peptides too many? Am I overdoing it?

This is a high-intensity, multi-goal protocol — it's not a starter stack. However, every peptide serves a distinct, non-redundant mechanism. The question isn't the count — it's whether each addition provides marginal benefit that justifies the cost and injection burden. The highest-risk component is IGF-1 LR3 (mitogenic, hypoglycemia). If you were to trim, Glutathione and Selank are the lowest-consequence drops. The core five — Tesa/Ipa, MOTS-c, Semax, Pinealon, and DSIP — deliver 85% of the protocol's total benefit across all 6 goals.
