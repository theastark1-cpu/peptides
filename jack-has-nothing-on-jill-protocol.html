Lifestyle Requirements (For Maximum Results)

  • Moderate calorie deficit or maintenance with high protein (1.6–2.2 g/kg) and high-fiber foods
  • Resistance training 3–4×/week + daily walking (aim for 8,000+ steps)
  • Regular blood sugar monitoring (fasting + post-meal)
  • 7–9 hours sleep, stress management, hydration + electrolytes
  • Track weight, waist circumference, energy, and glucose daily/weekly
Monitoring: Bloodwork at baseline, week 4, 8, and 12. Watch closely for GI sides, blood sugar changes, or injection reactions.

Detailed Benefits Breakdown & Supporting Evidence

Retatrutide (Triple Agonist)

Key benefits: Strong appetite control, significant fat loss (especially visceral), improved insulin sensitivity, and excellent blood sugar regulation. Ideal for pre-diabetes reversal.

Evidence: Phase 2/3 trials demonstrated up to 17–24% body weight loss with major improvements in A1C and metabolic markers.

BPC/TB Wolverine Blend

Key benefits: Repairs gut lining, reduces leaky gut and systemic inflammation (major driver of insulin resistance). TB-500 component adds tissue repair, angiogenesis, and overall recovery.

Evidence: Extensive preclinical studies (Sikiric et al. for BPC-157) on GI healing and anti-inflammatory effects combined with Thymosin Beta-4 research for tissue regeneration.

Stacking Synergy

  • Retatrutide = powerful metabolic fat-loss combination
  • BPC/TB Blend = essential gut and inflammation control for better insulin sensitivity
