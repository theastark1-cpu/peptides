07Lifestyle Requirements

  • Moderate calorie deficit or maintenance with high protein (1.6–2.2 g/kg) and high-fiber foods
  • Resistance training 3–4×/week + daily walking (aim for 8,000+ steps)
  • Regular blood sugar monitoring (fasting + post-meal)
  • 7–9 hours sleep, stress management, hydration + electrolytes
  • Track weight, waist circumference, energy, and glucose daily/weekly
Monitoring cadence: Bloodwork at baseline, week 4, 8, and 12. Watch closely for GI sides, blood sugar changes, or injection reactions.
